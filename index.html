Frequently Asked Any Questions

We're here to provide clarity and support during your legal journey. Below are answers to some of the most commonly asked questions by our clients.

1. Are application fee waivers available?

Yes, we understand that legal costs can be a barrier for many. That's why we offer fee waivers for clients who meet certain criteria. Whether you're facing financial hardship or working on a pro bono case, we encourage you to reach out to discuss your options. Our team is committed to making quality legal services accessible to everyone.

2. How do I schedule a consultation?

Scheduling a consultation with one of our experienced attorneys is easy. You can fill out our online contact form with your details, and one of our representatives will get back to you promptly to set up a meeting. Alternatively, feel free to call us directly during our working hours to speak with our team and book an appointment at your convenience.

3. What areas of law do you specialize in?

At Justice, we have a diverse team of legal experts who specialize in several key areas of law, including Business Law, Criminal Law, Child Law, Education Law, Divorce Law, and Tax Law. Our attorneys bring a wealth of experience and knowledge to each case, ensuring that you receive top-notch legal representation tailored to your specific needs.

4. Can I get legal advice online?

Absolutely. In today's digital age, we understand the importance of convenience and accessibility. That's why we offer online consultations where you can receive expert legal advice from the comfort of your home or office. Whether it's through a video call, email correspondence, or our secure client portal, we're here to assist you with your legal matters, no matter where you are. Reach out to us to schedule your virtual consultation.
